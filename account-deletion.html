Ledger Lala, operated by [Business/Owner Name], lets you request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to [contact email] from the email address registered on your Ledger Lala account.
  2. Use the subject line “Delete my Ledger Lala account”.
  3. Include the mobile number registered on your account so we can verify you.

We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days, and email you to confirm once it is done.

What data is deleted

On a verified request we permanently delete your account and personal data, including:

  • Your profile: name, mobile number, email, business/shop name, and address.
  • Your authentication account.
  • Records you own: retailers, ledger entries, replacements, orders, shortages, and disputes associated with your account.

What data may be kept

We may retain limited information where required by law (for example, tax or accounting obligations) or to prevent fraud and abuse. Such data is kept only as long as legally required, typically up to 7 years, and is then deleted.

Note: if other businesses you transact with on Ledger Lala have records that reference shared transactions, copies held by those businesses are governed by their own accounts.
