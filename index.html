Who we are

Ledger Lala is a business ledger app that helps distributors manage retailers, credit/debit entries, product replacements, orders, staff, and disputes, and lets retailers and staff view and act on that data. The App is operated by Tushar Gupta.

Information we collect

  • Account & profile: mobile number, email address, name, business/shop name, and address.
  • Business records you enter: retailers, credit/debit ledger entries and amounts, replacements, orders, shortages, and disputes — this may include financial information about your business relationships.
  • Staff data: name, mobile, email, designation, permissions for staff you add.
  • Device & push: a push notification identifier (via OneSignal) and basic technical data needed for the app to function.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, or advertising identifiers.

How we use your information

  • To provide core features: authentication, ledgers, replacements, orders, staff and catalog management, and statements/PDF exports.
  • To send notifications about activity relevant to you.
  • To secure accounts and prevent misuse.

We do not sell your personal information or use it for advertising.

How your data is stored and shared

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage). Push notifications are delivered through OneSignal. These providers process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • OneSignal — onesignal.com/privacy_policy

Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections and shared only with these providers or where required by law.

Data retention & deletion
